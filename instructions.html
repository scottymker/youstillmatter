    Frequently asked questions

    Do I need to keep the browser open for the app to work?

    No. Once YouStillMatter is installed it lives alongside your other apps. You can launch it offline and your saved check-ins, journal entries, and Calm Toolkit items stay on your device.

    Will installing the app cost anything?

    Never. The YouStillMatter progressive web app is free and respects your privacy—there are no hidden fees, ads, or data selling.

    How do I uninstall the app?

    You can remove it the same way you remove any other app. On mobile, press and hold the icon and choose Remove. On desktop, right click the app icon or open your browser's installed apps menu and choose Uninstall.

    The install option is missing—what can I try?

    Make sure you are using the latest version of your browser and that you visited https://app.youstillmatter.org. If the option still does not appear, you can add the page as a bookmark or pin the tab for quick access.
