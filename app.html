YouStillMatter App

Calm tools and crisis resources

Carry a compassionate companion everywhere you go. The YouStillMatter app brings grounding exercises, daily emotional check-ins, and immediate crisis connections together so you can steady your mind and reach help the moment you need it.

Join the waitlist See everything inside
Why download the app? Personalized calm plans that update with your mood. One-tap access to crisis lifelines, text lines, and local resources. Daily encouragement rooted in community stories of hope.

Feel grounded anywhere

Guided calm tools

  • Breathing, grounding, and sensory resets tailored to how you are feeling.
  • Quick-read instructions plus audio walk-throughs for when focus is hard.
  • Save your favorite tools for offline use when Wi-Fi or data is limited.

Mood-aware reminders

  • Set gentle reminders to pause, hydrate, move, or message a trusted contact.
  • Receive supportive nudges after difficult days or milestone achievements.
  • Track patterns with weekly reflections designed for you and your counselor.

Immediate crisis support

24/7 lifelines in one place

  • Call, text, or chat with national crisis teams including 988, Crisis Text Line, and SAMHSA.
  • Curated helplines for LGBTQ+, BIPOC, youth, and caregiver communities.
  • Geo-aware suggestions to surface local warm lines, shelters, and health clinics.

Safety planning made simple

  • Store coping steps, emergency contacts, and medication details securely on your device.
  • Share a safety plan PDF with loved ones or providers in seconds.
  • Automatic reminders to review and update your plan every season.

Community encouragement

Stories that reflect your journey

  • Read real stories submitted through the YouStillMatter platform to remember you are not alone.
  • Bookmark the words that resonate and return whenever you need encouragement.
  • Submit your own story anonymously to inspire someone else on their path.

Support circles

  • Create a private circle with friends, family, or mentors to share check-ins securely.
  • Send gentle "thinking of you" messages or practical offers of help.
  • Set shared goals—like weekly walks or therapy sessions—to celebrate progress together.

Be the first to know when we launch

We are building the YouStillMatter app with mental health advocates, youth advisors, and clinicians to ensure every feature feels supportive, trauma-informed, and inclusive.
