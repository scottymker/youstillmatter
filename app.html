  • Curated helplines for LGBTQ+, youth, caregivers, and more.
  • Your own Calm Toolkit contacts—one tap to text or call.

Need help right now?

You are not alone. If you’re in immediate danger, call 911.

  • United States: Call or text 988 • Text HOME to 741741
  • Canada: Call or text 988 • Text CONNECT to 686868
  • UK & Ireland: Samaritans 116 123 • Text SHOUT to 85258
