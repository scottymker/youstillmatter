Resources

If you’re hurting, help is real and reachable.

In a Crisis

  • US: Call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
  • Emergency: Call 911 if you’re in immediate danger.
  • Text: Text HOME to 741741 to reach a Crisis Counselor (US/Canada).

Helpful Links

  • NAMI—education, support, and advocacy.
  • SAMHSA—treatment locators and helplines.

We are not a crisis service. If you’re in danger or worried you might hurt yourself, please use the numbers above or your local emergency number.
